What is The Quote Tribune?

A randomized collection of the greatest quotes ever.

But the thing is, words are powerful, and have always been inspirational for the most beautiful minds in history. Quotes of the greatest must be heard and shared. And in order to make it even more powerful, we decided to add a mighty handsome photo of the author. Simply.
